# Privacy Policy for JineteApp

**Last Updated:** [17th December 2025]

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **JineteApp** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
-   **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, and student ID (for verification).
-   **Vehicle Information:** If you are a driver, we collect vehicle details (model, plate number) and verification documents (license, registration).
-   **User Content:** Feedback, ratings, and ride requests.

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically
-   **Location Data (Background & Foreground):** We collect your device's precise location to:
    -   Connect riders and drivers nearby.
    -   Show available rides on the map.
    -   Provide navigation instructions.
    -   **Note:** We may collect this data even when the app is in the background if you are actively sharing a ride or tracking a request.
-   **Device Information:** Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers.
-   **Usage Data:** How you interact with the app (screens visited, actions taken).

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
-   Facilitate ride-sharing services.
-   Verify your identity as a student or driver.
-   Improve app functionality and user experience.
-   Send important notifications regarding your rides.
-   Ensure safety and security.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We share data with trusted third-party services to operate the app:
-   **Google Maps API:** To provide map and location services.
-   **Firebase (Google):** For authentication, database storage, and analytics.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We may share information:
-   **With Other Users:** Riders and drivers see each other's name, rating, and location during a ride.
-   **For Legal Reasons:** If required by law or to protect our rights.

## 6. Your Rights
You have the right to:
-   Access your personal data.
-   Request deletion of your account and data.
-   Opt-out of location tracking (though this will limit app functionality).
